Landscaping Services Across Farnborough & Hampshire

Glorious Gardens Son & Co. is a trusted, family-run landscaping company serving homeowners throughout Farnborough, Hampshire, Surrey and the surrounding areas. From simple patio upgrades to complete garden makeovers, we handle every project with the same level of craftsmanship and attention to detail.

Patio Installation

We install porcelain, natural stone and block paving patios across Farnborough and Hampshire. Every patio starts with a proper sub-base so it stays level and lasts for decades. Whether you want a simple seating area or a full outdoor entertaining space, we deliver it on time and on budget.

Patio Installation →

Artificial Grass

Our artificial grass installations look and feel like natural turf — without the mud, watering or mowing. Ideal for families with children and pets, we use premium blades with proper drainage layers so the lawn performs in all weathers. Installations typically take 1–3 days with zero mess left behind.

Artificial Grass →

Composite Decking

Composite decking lasts 25+ years with virtually no maintenance — no staining, no splitting and no annual treatments. We install leading brands in a range of colours and finishes, with optional glass balustrades, built-in lighting and privacy screens to create a complete outdoor room.

Composite Decking →

Garden Lighting

Professional LED garden lighting transforms your outdoor space after dark — highlighting plants, illuminating pathways and creating ambience for entertaining. We design and install low-voltage systems that are safe, energy-efficient and controllable from your smartphone.

Garden Lighting →

Driveways

From traditional block paving to resin-bound and pattern-imprinted concrete, we install driveways that add serious kerb appeal. All our driveway work uses proper sub-base and drainage to meet SuDS regulations, and we handle planning and kerb drops from start to finish.

Driveways →

Full Garden Makeovers

Our full garden makeover service combines every element — patio, lawn, decking, planting, lighting and fencing — into one seamlessly managed project. We handle design, groundworks, materials and installation so you have a single point of contact from first survey to final handover.
