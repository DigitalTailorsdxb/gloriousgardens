Our Process

A clear, transparent process from first contact to project handover.

1

Free AI Quote

Upload a photo of your current driveway and get an instant price and realistic preview of your new surface — free, no obligation.

2

Site Survey

We assess the size, existing drainage, crossover condition and edging requirements. We'll advise on the best material for your specific property.

3

Excavation & Sub-Base

Existing surface and topsoil are removed. A compacted MOT Type 1 sub-base of 100–150mm is installed — the foundation of a long-lasting driveway.

4

Edging Installation

Concrete kerb edgings or block soldier courses are laid to retain the surface and provide a clean, defined edge.

5

Surface Installation

Your chosen material is installed and compacted or bound as required. Drainage channels and gullies are fitted where needed.

6

Finishing & Clean-Up

Jointing compound or kiln-dried sand is brushed in, the surface is compacted and sealed if required. Site fully cleared before handover.
