Yes — we strongly recommend sealing Indian sandstone on installation and every 2–3 years thereafter. Sealing prevents staining, reduces moss growth and extends the life of the patio significantly.
Properly laid and maintained sandstone can last 30–50 years. The key factors are a well-prepared base, correct jointing compound and regular sealing.
Riven (natural textured) sandstone has good slip resistance. Honed or smooth finishes can be slippery when wet so we advise against these for main patio areas.
We offer random mixed sizes (600×900, 600×600, 450×600, 450×450) or uniform sizing. Mixed random lay is the most popular as it gives a natural, authentic look.
We carry a wide range of sandstone types and can often achieve a very close match to existing slabs. Bring a sample to your survey and we'll source the closest available option.
