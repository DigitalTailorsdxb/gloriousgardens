Our Story

How we became the trusted name in landscaping

We started Glorious Gardens to do things properly — no shortcuts, no rushed jobs. Just honest work, solid results, and gardens that people are proud to come home to. Lorenzo & Esat have been working side by side for over 25 years, building a reputation in Farnborough and across Hampshire & Surrey that we're truly proud of.

Every project we take on starts with a conversation, not a sales pitch. We want to understand exactly how you use your outdoor space, what you love about it, and what you wish was different. We then deliver a solution built to last.

Today, we combine that same hands-on family craftsmanship with cutting-edge AI technology — offering instant quotes and photorealistic design visualisations that make professional landscaping accessible to every homeowner.

Our Approach to Every Project

Every garden we work on starts with a conversation — not a sales pitch. We want to understand how you actually use your outdoor space, what frustrates you about it now, and what you dream it could become. Only then do we design a solution.

We believe the best landscaping work is invisible in the sense that it feels completely natural to the home and garden it belongs to. We don't impose a house style. We don't upsell materials you don't need. We listen, design properly, and then build it to last.

Our AI-powered instant quote tool — built in partnership with Trade Engine — means you can get an accurate price indication and a photorealistic design visualisation in 90 seconds, completely free, before committing to anything. We introduced this because we know that uncertainty about cost is the biggest reason people put off garden projects for years. We wanted to remove that barrier.
