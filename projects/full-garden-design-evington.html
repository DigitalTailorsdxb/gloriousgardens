Project Overview

This Evington homeowner had recently completed a rear extension and wanted the garden to match the quality and finish of their newly renovated home. The existing garden was a blank canvas — stripped back to bare earth by the builders — giving us the rare opportunity to design from scratch.

The brief centred on creating a garden that felt like a true extension of the interior: clean lines, quality materials, and a design that could be enjoyed from inside the house as well as out. The homeowner also wanted low maintenance at the forefront of every decision.

We used our AI design tool to generate three concept directions based on their mood board references, which the customer then refined into a final brief. The result is a contemporary, well-structured garden with clear zones — dining, lawn, and planting — that flows beautifully from the new bifold doors.

  • 8-light LED garden lighting system

How We Did It

A step-by-step look at the transformation process.

1

Design Consultation

Three AI-generated concept directions were presented based on the customer's brief. The contemporary minimal scheme was selected and developed into full working drawings.

2

Site Clearance & Prep

The site was cleared, levelled and drainage assessed. New close-board fencing was installed to all three boundaries before any hard landscaping began.

3

Groundworks

Sub-bases were constructed for both the patio and lawn zones. A linear drainage channel was installed at the patio threshold. All levels were set to drain away from the property.

4

Patio Installation

600×900mm porcelain slabs were laid in a staggered bond with a single-step riser to the raised border zone. All joints pointed with flexible light grey compound.

5

Lawn, Beds & Lighting

Artificial grass was laid to the lawn zone with shaped edges. Hardwood sleeper raised beds were constructed and filled. The 8-light LED system was installed and commissioned.

6

Planting & Handover

The ornamental planting scheme was installed across the raised beds and borders. The garden was cleaned, photographed and handed over with all documentation.

The Results

The Evington project is one of our most photographed — and it's easy to see why. The combination of large-format porcelain, curved artificial lawn and sleeper-edged raised beds creates a garden with genuine visual depth and interest.

The homeowner commented that the finished garden had exceeded the brief entirely, and that they now spend more time in the garden than any other room of the house during the summer months.

Completed in 10 days. 5-star review and two referrals followed within a month of completion.
