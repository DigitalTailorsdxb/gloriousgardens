Project Overview

This Narborough family had a 55m² rear lawn that had become a constant source of frustration. Heavy clay soil meant the lawn held water for days after any rainfall, leaving it waterlogged and unusable during autumn and winter. Bare patches and compacted areas made it unsafe for their two young children to play on.

The family had considered re-turfing but were concerned about the ongoing maintenance and the same drainage problems recurring. After using our free AI design tool to see a preview, they decided artificial grass was the right solution.

We installed a premium 35mm pile artificial turf over a compacted crushed stone sub-base with a commercial weed membrane. The result is a lush, even lawn that drains freely, stays green year-round and requires zero maintenance.

Materials Used

35mm Premium Artificial Turf MOT Type 1 Sub-base Weed Suppression Membrane Galvanised Fixing Nails Flexible Edge Banding Kiln-Dried Sand Infill

Scope of Work

  • Removal of existing lawn, weeds and 100mm topsoil
  • All excavated material removed from site
  • 50mm compacted crushed stone sub-base
  • Commercial weed suppression membrane
  • Premium 35mm artificial turf installation
  • Shaped edges to curved borders
  • Infill with kiln-dried silica sand

How We Did It

A step-by-step look at the transformation process.

1

Consultation & AI Preview

The homeowner used our free AI quote tool to upload a photo and see a realistic preview of their new lawn before committing. This gave them full confidence in the end result.

2

Lawn Removal & Excavation

The existing lawn, weeds and 100mm of topsoil were stripped and removed. All material was taken off-site. Edges were cut cleanly to the border lines.

3

Sub-Base & Membrane

A 50mm layer of MOT Type 1 crushed stone was spread and compacted with a vibrating plate. A commercial-grade weed suppression membrane was laid over the full area.

4

Turf Installation

The artificial turf was rolled out, cut to shape and secured with galvanised pins at 150mm centres around all edges. Joins were taped and brushed to ensure invisible seams.

5

Infill & Edging

Kiln-dried silica sand was brushed into the pile to add weight, stability and a natural feel underfoot. Flexible aluminium edge banding was fitted to all border edges.

6

Final Inspection

The completed lawn was power-brushed to lift the pile, inspected for any imperfections, and handed over with a care guide and 10-year manufacturer warranty documentation.

The Results

The Narborough family now have a garden their children use every single day — regardless of the weather. The waterlogging problem is completely resolved, and the lawn looks as lush in January as it does in July.

The mother of the family commented: "We wish we'd done this years ago. The kids are out there all the time now, and I'm not constantly mopping muddy footprints."
