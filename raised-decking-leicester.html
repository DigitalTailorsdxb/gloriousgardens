Raised Decking Leicester — Frequently Asked Questions

Raised decks over 300mm from ground level may require planning permission depending on their proximity to boundaries and the property. We advise on this during the survey and can assist with applications if needed.
We build raised decks from 300mm up to 2m+ high where ground levels require it. Structural calculations are provided for all decks over 600mm and handrails are included where required for safety.
A standard raised deck takes 3–7 days depending on size, height and complexity. Adding balustrades, stairs or integrated lighting will add time to the build.
Composite boards require almost no maintenance — no annual oiling or staining needed. Hardwood looks stunning but needs annual oiling to maintain its colour. Softwood is the most economical but requires more regular treatment.
