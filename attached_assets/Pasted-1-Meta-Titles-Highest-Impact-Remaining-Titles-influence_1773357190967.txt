1. Meta Titles (Highest Impact Remaining)

Titles influence rankings and click-through rate.

Target structure:

Primary Keyword + Location | Secondary Keyword | Brand

Example service page:

Porcelain Patio Installation Leicester | Patio Specialists | Premium Landscapes

Example location page:

Landscapers in Wigston | Patios, Decking & Garden Design | Premium Landscapes

Rules:

• 55–60 characters ideal
• keyword at beginning
• location included
• avoid duplication across pages

2. Meta Descriptions (CTR Optimisation)

Descriptions do not directly affect ranking, but they influence clicks which indirectly improves performance.

Structure:

Service + Location + Trust + CTA

Example:

Professional landscapers in Leicester specialising in patios, artificial grass and composite decking. Get an instant garden quote and design online in under 90 seconds.

Length:

150–160 characters

Every page must have a unique description.

3. Heading Structure (Critical for SEO)

Each page should follow:

H1 – Main keyword
H2 – Service sections
H3 – Details

Example patio page:

H1: Patio Installation Leicester

H2: Porcelain Patio Installation
H2: Natural Stone Patios
H2: Patio Design Options
H2: Patio Installation Cost Leicester
H2: Recent Patio Projects
H2: FAQs

Avoid multiple H1s.

4. Image SEO (Huge Missed Opportunity)

Landscaping sites have hundreds of images but almost no optimisation.

Every image should have:

Filename

Bad:

IMG_4822.jpg

Good:

porcelain-patio-leicester.jpg
Alt Tag
alt="Porcelain patio installation in Leicester garden"

This helps you rank in Google Image search, which landscapers often convert from.

5. Internal Linking (Major Ranking Factor)

Every service page should link to:

• 3 other services
• 2 location pages
• homepage

Example inside patio page:

Looking for artificial grass installation in Leicester? View our artificial grass service.

Google uses internal linking to understand topic authority.

6. Add FAQ Schema (Easy Ranking Boost)

Add FAQ sections like:

How much does a patio cost in Leicester?
What is the best material for patios?
How long does patio installation take?
Do you offer garden design?

Then apply FAQ schema markup.

This can create Google rich results.

7. Add LocalBusiness Schema

Add structured data like:

LandscapeDesigner
HomeAndConstructionBusiness

Include:

name
address
telephone
opening hours
service area
reviews

This helps Google associate your site with your Google Business listing.

8. Add Breadcrumb Navigation

Example:

Home > Landscaping > Patios > Leicester

Benefits:

• better crawl structure
• improved indexing
• enhanced search snippets

9. Improve Homepage Content

Your homepage should include 800–1200 words total.

Sections:

Landscaping Services in Leicester
Patio Installation
Artificial Grass Installation
Composite Decking
Garden Design
Full Garden Transformations

This allows Google to understand your core service offering.

10. Add Location Signals Across Site

Mention locations naturally in content:

Example:

We install patios across Leicester including Wigston, Oadby, Narborough and Blaby.

This helps rank for nearby searches.

11. Add a “Recent Projects” Section

Google values real work examples.

Create:

/projects
/projects/porcelain-patio-leicester
/projects/artificial-grass-wigston
/projects-composite-decking-narborough

Include:

• before/after photos
• materials
• cost range
• garden size

These pages rank extremely well.

12. Speed Optimisation

Targets:

Mobile score > 85
LCP under 2.5 seconds

Actions:

• convert images to WebP
• lazy load images
• compress CSS/JS
• reduce heavy animations

garden landscaping cost calculator
