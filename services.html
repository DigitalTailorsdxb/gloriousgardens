Patio & Paving

A well-built patio is the most-used feature in any Farnborough garden — and the one that adds the most immediate value. We install porcelain, natural stone and block paving patios with a fully compacted sub-base, professional pointing, and a proper drainage gradient. From a simple garden seating area to a multi-level entertaining terrace, we design and build patios that last 20+ years with minimal upkeep.

Decking

Composite decking outperforms timber in every practical way — no annual oiling, no green algae, no warping or splinters, and a structural warranty of up to 25 years. We install composite boards on a galvanised or pressure-treated sub-frame, using hidden clip systems for a completely screw-free surface. Choose from a wide range of wood-effect and contemporary finishes to suit any Farnborough garden style.

  • Composite decking
  • Hardwood timber decking
  • Multi-level designs
  • Built-in seating & planters

Driveways

A new driveway is one of the highest-ROI improvements you can make to a Hampshire property — immediately improving kerb appeal and adding real market value. We install block paving, resin bound gravel and porcelain driveways, all fully compliant with Hampshire planning requirements for permeable surfaces. Every installation includes a properly compacted sub-base and correct drainage to ensure a 20+ year lifespan.
