Limestone performs well outdoors in the UK when sealed correctly. We use outdoor-grade limestone with a suitable textured or riven finish to ensure adequate grip in wet conditions.
Limestone is more porous than porcelain and can stain if spills aren't cleaned promptly. A quality sealant dramatically reduces this risk and makes maintenance much easier.
Limestone has a more natural, organic appearance while porcelain is more uniform and requires less maintenance. Limestone typically costs slightly less but needs sealing every 2–3 years.
We use 22–30mm limestone for outdoor patio applications to ensure adequate strength and frost resistance. Thinner tiles are only suitable for indoor use.
