Composite vs Timber — Is It Worth Paying More?

🔷 Composite Decking

  • ✅ No annual oiling or staining
  • ✅ Won't warp, splinter or rot
  • ✅ 20–25 year board warranties
  • ✅ Consistent colour and finish
  • ✅ Slip-resistant grooved surface
  • ❌ Higher upfront cost
  • ❌ Can feel less "natural" than timber

🟫 Timber Decking

  • ✅ Natural warm appearance
  • ✅ Lower upfront cost
  • ✅ Easy to repair individual boards
  • ✅ Can be sanded and re-stained
  • ❌ Requires annual oiling/staining
  • ❌ Can warp, split and splinter
  • ❌ Prone to algae and mould if neglected
