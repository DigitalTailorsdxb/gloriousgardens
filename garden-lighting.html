Garden Lighting

Transform Your Garden After Dark

Extend the use of your garden into the evening with beautifully designed outdoor lighting. From subtle pathway lights to dramatic feature uplighting, we make your garden shine.

FREE Get Instant Quote & AI Design

Garden lighting is one of the most impactful and cost-effective ways to transform your outdoor space. A well-lit garden not only extends the hours you can use it, but also enhances security, adds kerb appeal and creates a dramatic atmosphere for entertaining.

Premium Landscapes designs and installs garden lighting systems across Leicester, the Midlands and Home Counties. We specialise in low-voltage LED systems — energy-efficient, safe to install underground, and available in a huge range of styles and colour temperatures.

From a simple pathway kit to a fully designed, app-controlled smart lighting system, we tailor every installation to your garden's unique layout and your personal style. Get a free instant quote and AI garden design preview using our online tool.

Our Process

A clear, transparent process from first contact to project handover.

1

Design Consultation

We discuss your vision, walk the garden after dark if needed, and design a lighting scheme that highlights your best features.

2

Free AI Quote

Get an instant price estimate and visualisation of your lighting design using our AI-powered quote tool — free, no obligation.

3

Cable Routing Plan

We plan the most efficient cable routes, minimising disruption to planting and paving. All cables run through conduit for future access.

4

Transformer & Control Install

A weatherproof low-voltage transformer is installed, along with any smart control systems or timers.

5

Light Installation

All lights are positioned, connected and tested. Spike lights and bollards are carefully placed for best effect.

6

Commissioning & Demo

We set your preferred scenes and timers, and demonstrate the system fully before handover.
