Key Factors That Affect Landscaping Costs

📍 Location

Labour costs in Leicester and the Midlands are typically 15–25% lower than London and the South East. Material costs are similar nationally.

📐 Garden Size

Larger projects cost more in total but less per m². Fixed costs (skips, groundworks setup, delivery) spread over a larger area.

🏗️ Ground Conditions

Clay soil, old concrete, tree roots and poor drainage all add to groundworks cost. A good landscaper will assess this before quoting.

✨ Material Grade

Porcelain costs twice as much as concrete slabs. Premium composite decking costs three times budget softwood. Material choice is the biggest cost driver.

📅 Time of Year

Spring and early summer are peak season — lead times are longer and some contractors charge a premium. Autumn and winter work often comes with better availability and pricing.

🔧 Project Complexity

Curved edges, steps, raised areas, drainage and lighting all add complexity. Simple square patios cost the least per m²; complex multi-level gardens cost the most.
