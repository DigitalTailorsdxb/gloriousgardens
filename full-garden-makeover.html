Complete Garden Transformation — Start to Finish

A full garden makeover is exactly what it sounds like — a complete transformation of your outdoor space from the ground up. No piecemeal improvements. No coordinating multiple contractors. Just one team, one plan, and one outstanding result.

Premium Landscapes manages full garden makeovers across Leicester, the Midlands and Home Counties. We handle every element in-house: design, groundworks, patio or decking, artificial grass or planting, boundary treatments, garden lighting and finishing details.

The process begins with our free AI-powered design tool. Upload a photo of your current garden, describe your vision, and receive a photorealistic preview of your redesigned space within 90 seconds — completely free, no obligation. When you're ready to proceed, we handle everything else.

Our Process

A clear, transparent process from first contact to project handover.

1

Free AI Design Preview

Upload your garden photo and describe your dream space. Receive a photorealistic AI design preview in 90 seconds — completely free.

2

Full Design Consultation

We visit, survey and discuss your vision in detail. A full design is produced showing all elements of the makeover.

3

Detailed Quotation

A transparent, itemised quote covering every element of the project. No hidden costs, no surprises.

4

Clearance & Groundworks

The existing garden is fully cleared. Ground is levelled, drainage installed and sub-bases laid for all hard surfaces.

5

Hard Landscaping

Patio, decking, paths, steps and boundaries are built to the design. This is where the transformation begins.

6

Soft Landscaping & Lighting

Lawns, planting and lighting are installed to complete the design. The garden is cleaned, snag-checked and handed over.
